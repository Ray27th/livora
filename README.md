# One59.com

Singapore furniture e-commerce brand with a mixed evergreen and drops catalog, GST-included pricing, mainland Singapore delivery only, and a social-proof-heavy storefront.

## Canonical docs

- `one59-spec.md`, canonical product specification
- `one59-ecommerce.md`, execution-focused build plan

## Contributor-safe principles

- Treat `one59-spec.md` as the source of truth
- Keep prices under S$159 per item, but don't force basket totals under that cap
- Keep shipping flat for MVP, with standard delivery at 3 to 5 days
- Keep the order flow to the three states defined in the spec
- Do not add pickup, express shipping, or post-payment cancellations
- Keep product data aligned to category templates and separate SKUs for variants
